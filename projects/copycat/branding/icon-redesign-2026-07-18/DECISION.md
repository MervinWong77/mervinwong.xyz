# CopyCat icon redesign — decision

## Rejected (previous shipping icon)
Twin white overlapping cat **silhouettes** on teal — abstract blob at Dock size; no clear “cat face” or “copy/files” story.

## Concepts (distinct directions)

### A — Cat face + twin documents *(preferred)*
AI exploration + production master. Teal cat face, white eyes, two white sheets behind.
- **16/32:** size-optimized geometric master (chunky ears, separated white eyes, clear paper stack)
- **64–1024:** photographic/3D master downscales
- **Verdict: SHIP**

### B — Twin overlapping cat heads
Duplicate metaphor without documents.
- Soft twin collapses toward one shape below 32px
- Weaker “files/duplicates” signal
- **Reject**

### C — Cat peeking over documents
- Below 32px becomes a document blob with a bump
- **Reject**

### D — Cat face + document badge
Exploration; badge detail fails small
- **Reject**

## Winner
**Option A — simple cat face with two overlapping documents on a teal macOS squircle.**
